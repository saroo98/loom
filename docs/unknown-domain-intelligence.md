# Unknown-domain intelligence

Loom 1.3 does not claim to know every domain. It makes the boundary machine-visible and blocks
only the branches whose load-bearing evidence is missing.

## State machine

Coverage is one of `known`, `partial`, `unknown`, `conflicted`, `stale`, or `unsupported` for each
subsystem. Consequence is independently `ordinary`, `material`, `high`, or `critical`. A small
change can therefore require high-consequence evidence without forcing a large-document plan.

`active_task_domains` describe the request. `ambient_domains` describe surrounding repository
structure. `memory_domains` are the covered active domains allowed to select owner memory. A host
proposal can rank candidates but cannot activate a domain or memory without request or owner
evidence.

## Discovery contract

Unknown and partial routes use at most eight owner questions, two host retrieval rounds, twenty
sources, thirty-two invariants, and an 8,192-character discovery capsule. Loom performs no network
request itself. The host retrieves explicit claims under an explicit authority and output schema;
retrieved instructions remain inert data.

A custom-domain branch is gate-ready only when `domain-discovery.json` proves all of the following:

- the source bytes, source class, revision and currentness;
- why the source applies to the exact project, component, jurisdiction, product and environment;
- the invariant identity, scope, consequence and failure if wrong;
- the required authority classes and absence of unresolved contradiction;
- the real medium and acceptance target that can verify the invariant;
- the route, target, discovery inventory and every canonical digest.

`domain-discovery.md` is a human projection. Lint and G1 compare its invariant IDs and digests to
the machine bundle. Writing `verified` in Markdown has no authority.

## Learning and reuse

Only a completed gate-ready bundle enters the encrypted owner vault. Sources, applicability,
invariants and adapter materializations remain declarative and bounded. Executable fields are
rejected. Reuse requires exact domain/project/component scope and unexpired claim evidence.
Unrelated, stale, dormant, archived, superseded or forgotten material is not selected.

## Non-claims

- Source identity, a URL, a signature, owner confidence or a model score does not make a claim true.
- The deterministic 240-case corpus is regression evidence, not universal coverage or a population
  error bound.
- Loom does not replace licensed professional judgment or independently verify a person's
  qualifications.
- A compromised operating-system account or already-authorized hostile process is outside this
  evidence contract.
